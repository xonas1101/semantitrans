ASR ROBUSTNESS: WORD ERROR RATE vs CHANNEL SNR (noise_eval.py)
==============================================================
Plot: wer_snr.png   Data: wer_snr.csv   (n = 60 clips)

Additive white Gaussian noise (AWGN) is injected into the test-set audio at a
range of SNRs to simulate transmission over a progressively worse wireless
channel; Whisper ASR (stage 1) is re-run on the degraded audio.

  SNR (dB)   mean WER
   clean       0.10
    30         0.12
    20         0.15
    10         0.37
     5         0.60
     0         0.83
    -5         1.05

TAKEAWAYS
- Clean-audio WER is ~10%; the curve rises monotonically as the channel
  degrades, passing 100% (more errors than reference words) at -5 dB.
- This motivates the semantic-communication experiment (semcom_summary.txt):
  if the raw signal is this fragile, transmit the extracted MEANING instead.
